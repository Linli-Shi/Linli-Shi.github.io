My name is Linli Shi. I am a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zürich and UniDistance Suisse , working with Prof. David Loeffler (ERC Consolidator Grant) and Prof. Sarah Zerbes .

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Connecticut under the supervision of Prof. Liang Xiao.

My research interests include number theory, automorphic forms and arithmetic geometry. I have been working on topics related to Beilinson's conjectures, Euler systems, Kudla program and relative Langlands program.

You can find more information about me in my CV. You can reach me by linli.shi@math.ethz.ch.
