My name is Linli Shi. I am a Ph.D. student in Department of Mathematics at the University of Connecticut. My advisor is Prof. Liang Xiao.

My research interests include number theory, automorphic forms and arithmetic geometry. I have been working on topics related to Beilinson's conjectures, Kudla program and relative Langlands program.

You can find more information about me in my CV. You can reach me by linli.shi@uconn.edu. I always welcome any mathematical or general discussions.
